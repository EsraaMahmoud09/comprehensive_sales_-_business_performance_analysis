##  Dashboard Insights & Business Recommendations

This section summarizes the key insights derived from the dashboard visuals, along with actionable business recommendations.

---
##  Tools & Technologies Used

This project was developed using the following tools and technologies:

* Microsoft Excel
  Used for building the interactive dashboard, data visualization, and overall analysis.

* **Power Query**
  Used for data cleaning and transformation, including:

  * Removing duplicates
  * Handling missing values
  * Fixing data types
  * Structuring the dataset for analysis

* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**
  Used to create dynamic calculations and measures such as:

  * Total Sales
  * Number of Transactions
  * KPIs and performance metrics
  * Applying row context and filter context

These tools enabled efficient data preparation, advanced analysis, and dynamic reporting.

#  1. Customer & Operational Insights

###  Account Status Analysis

The dashboard highlights a noticeable proportion of **inactive accounts**, which indicates a potentially high **customer churn rate**.

** Insight:**
Inactive customers represent lost revenue opportunities and reduced customer engagement.

** Recommendation:**

* Launch targeted **retargeting campaigns** to re-engage inactive users
* Offer incentives such as discounts, cashback, or loyalty rewards
* Implement automated notifications to encourage account activity

---

###  Demographic Distribution

The analysis shows that **younger customers** form the largest segment of active users.

** Insight:**
This indicates a strong preference for digital and mobile-based financial services.

** Recommendation:**

* Enhance **Mobile Banking** features and user experience
* Invest in digital payment solutions (e-wallets, instant transfers)
* Design marketing campaigns tailored to younger audiences

---

#  2. Executive & Employee Performance

###  Employee Workload Analysis

The dashboard reveals a significant gap between **top-performing employees** and others in terms of transaction volume.

** Insight:**
This imbalance suggests inefficient workload distribution and potential performance gaps.

** Recommendation:**

* Implement **workload balancing strategies**
* Provide targeted **training programs** for underperforming employees
* Introduce performance monitoring systems and KPIs

---

###  Branch Efficiency

Some branches significantly outperform others despite having similar staffing levels.

** Insight:**
Performance differences are likely driven by factors such as location, management efficiency, or customer base.

** Recommendation:**

* Analyze high-performing branches to identify success factors
* Replicate best practices across other branches
* Optimize branch operations and resource allocation

---

#  3. Product & Profitability Analysis

###  Transaction Channels (Media)

The dashboard indicates that **Mobile Banking** is the most frequently used channel, while **ATM transactions** still experience high demand for cash withdrawals.

** Insight:**
Customers prefer digital channels, but reliance on ATMs remains high.

** Recommendation:**

* Increase ATM availability in high-demand areas
* Encourage digital transactions to reduce operational pressure
* Promote cashless services through incentives

---

###  Service Success Rate

Certain services (e.g., loans) show higher failure rates or longer processing times.

** Insight:**
This reflects inefficiencies in service workflows and potential customer dissatisfaction.

** Recommendation:**

* Simplify approval processes and reduce manual steps
* Implement **automation** to minimize human error
* Improve service-level agreements (SLAs)

---

#  Overall Business Impact

The dashboard demonstrates how data analysis can uncover:

* Customer behavior patterns
* Operational inefficiencies
* Product performance gaps
* Data quality issues

By acting on these insights, organizations can:

* Improve customer retention
* Enhance operational efficiency
* Increase profitability
* Ensure data reliability for decision-making
